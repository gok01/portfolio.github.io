Title: Smart Blind Stick

Description: Smart Blind Stick is an Arduino based project which works on the concept of ultrasonic signals. Ultrasonic are the signals that reflects on hitting the objects or surfaces. It was designed to aid visually impaired people. The module contains a processor, a buzzer and a vibration motor. The intensity of the vibration increases with decrease in the distance between the sensor and the obstacle. The calculation behind the working is the time taken by the signal to reach back. Using the time taken and the speed known the distance is found and used in the working.
